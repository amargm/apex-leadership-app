INTRODUCTION

APEX Leadership App ("APEX", "we", "us", "our") is developed and operated by Amar Mugali. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play.

By using APEX, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

A. Information you provide directly:

  • Google account information (display name, email address, profile photo URL) when you sign in with Google
  • Personal notes you create within the app

B. Information collected automatically:

  • Lesson progress and completion status
  • Reading statistics (day streak, time spent reading, cases completed)
  • Saved/bookmarked lessons
  • App preferences (font size, theme settings)
  • Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)

C. Information we do NOT collect:

  • Device contacts, phone number, or SMS
  • Location data (GPS, network-based)
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information (handled entirely by Google Play)
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and maintain the learning service
  • To sync your progress across devices via your Google account
  • To display personalized learning statistics
  • To restore your data if you reinstall the app
  • To identify and fix crashes and improve app stability

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their commercial purposes.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

APEX uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — to manage sign-in. Privacy Policy
  • Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) — to store your progress and notes securely. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — to collect anonymous crash reports for stability improvements. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — required for authentication and in-app purchases. Privacy Policy

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore servers, secured by Firebase Authentication. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Only your authenticated session can access your data — no other user or administrator can view your personal notes or progress.

GUEST USERS

If you use APEX as a guest (without signing in), no personal data is collected or transmitted. All progress and notes remain stored locally on your device only and are not backed up to any server.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your data via the "Reset All Progress" feature, it is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. If you do not use the app for 24 consecutive months, we may delete your stored data after sending a notification to your registered email.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — visible in-app (progress, notes, stats)
  • Delete your data — use "Reset All Progress" in Profile settings, or contact us for full cloud deletion
  • Withdraw consent — sign out and use the app as a guest at any time
  • Data portability — contact us to request an export of your data

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

APEX is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence (Google Cloud infrastructure). By using APEX, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations, which may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
