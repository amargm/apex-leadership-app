PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 3, 2026

OVERVIEW

APEX ("we", "our", "the app") is a leadership learning application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your display name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile photo URL

We also store your in-app data:

  • Lesson progress and completion status
  • Personal notes you create
  • Saved lessons and preferences
  • Reading statistics (streak, time spent)

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To sync your learning progress across devices
  • To provide personalized learning statistics
  • To restore your data if you reinstall the app

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by Google's authentication service.

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, secured by Firebase Authentication. Only you can access your data through your authenticated session.

GUEST USERS

If you use the app as a guest, no data is collected or stored remotely. All data remains on your device only.

DATA DELETION

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Progress" option in the Profile screen. You can also sign out, which removes local data. To request complete deletion of cloud data, contact us.

CONTACT

For privacy-related questions, contact: apex.leadership.app@gmail.com
