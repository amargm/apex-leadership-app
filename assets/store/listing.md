## Full Description (4000 chars max)

APEX is a leadership learning app built for people who want to grow — fast.

Each case study breaks down a pivotal leadership decision from the world's most iconic companies: Netflix's culture bet, Nadella's empathy revolution at Microsoft, SpaceX's three-failure comeback, Apple's product-killing rebirth, and more.

No fluff. No 400-page books. Just the decision, the context, the outcome, and what you can take from it.

▸ HOW IT WORKS

Every case study has four tabs:
• OVERVIEW — The core leadership insight in 2 minutes
• TIMELINE — The key moments that made the decision matter
• REFLECT — A prompt to connect the lesson to your own leadership
• TAKEAWAYS — Actionable principles you can use immediately
